Maratha Empire

The Maratha Empire, also referred to
as the Maratha Confederacy, was an
early modern indian confederation that rose to dominate much of the Indian subcontinent in the 18th century. Maratha rule formally began in 1674 with the coronation of Shivaji of the Bhonsle Dynasty as the Chhatrapati.

Quantum mechanics

Quantum mechanics is a fundamental theory in physics in that provides a description of the physical properties of nature at the scale of atom and subatomic particles. it is the foundation of all quantum physics including quantum chemistry, quantum field theory,quantum technology, and quantum information science.
